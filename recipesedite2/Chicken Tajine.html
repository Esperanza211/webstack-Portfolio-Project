chicken tajine

Ingredients

  • 31 whole chicken (cut into pieces) or 4-6 chicken thighs or drumsticks 2 large onions, finely chopped 3-4 cloves garlic, minced 1-2 preserved lemons, quartered and seeds removed 1/4 cup olive oil 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 1 cup green or black olives 1/2 cup water or chicken broth Spices: 1 teaspoon ground ginger 1 teaspoon ground turmeric 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika (optional) 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (optional, for heat) Salt to taste Optional Ingredients: 2-3 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced 2-3 carrots, peeled and sliced 1 cup green beans, trimmed 1/2 cup dried apricots or prunes (for a sweet touch) 1-2 tablespoons honey (for a sweet touch) 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice (for extra tanginess)

Instructions

Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with olive oil, garlic, ginger, turmeric, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, black pepper, and salt. Toss to coat the chicken evenly. Cover and let it marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight in the refrigerator for best results. Prepare the Tagine: Heat a large tagine or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat. Add a little olive oil and saut√© the onions until they become soft and translucent. Cook the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the tagine or pot. Brown the chicken pieces on all sides for about 5-7 minutes. Add the chopped cilantro and parsley, preserved lemons, olives, and water or chicken broth. If using, add the potatoes, carrots, green beans, dried apricots, or prunes. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer gently for about 45-60 minutes. Check occasionally to ensure there is enough liquid; add more water or broth if necessary. The chicken should be tender and cooked through, and the vegetables should be soft. Finishing Touches: Adjust the seasoning with salt, lemon juice, and honey if desired. Garnish with additional fresh cilantro and parsley before serving. Serve: Serve the chicken tagine hot, accompanied by Moroccan bread (khobz) or couscous.
